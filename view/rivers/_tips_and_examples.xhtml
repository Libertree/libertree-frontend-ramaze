Tips:

  • For the search, enter one or more words or hashtags.
  • Posts having any of the words will be included in the river.
  • Use a minus sign in front of a word to exclude posts with that word. Example: #music -#classical will find posts about music, but not classical music.
  • Use the special term :unread to include only posts which you have not read yet.
  • Use the special term :tree to include only posts by members of the same tree as you.
  • Use double quotes to match multi-word phrases, or exact matches involving symbols.
  • Use the special term :from followed by a name in quotes to match all posts by that person. Example: :from "Linus Torvalds"
  • You can also have the beginner rivers recreated for you.

Examples

Query Description
:tree All posts originating from your tree
:forest All posts transmitted to your tree from every other tree in the forest, plus posts from your tree
:unread #knitting Posts tagged with #knitting which you have not read yet
#funny -#cats -#lolcats Posts tagged #funny, but not including posts about cats
environment Posts that mention the environment, whether or not they were hashtagged with #environment
:tree #bug #feature Bug reports and feature requests made by your treemates
"new york" Posts about New York, but not posts only having the word new
brazil -"world cup" Posts about Brazil, but not those mentioning the World Cup
:from "rekado" Posts written by rekado
#photography -:from "Photophile" Posts tagged with #photography, but not those by Photophile
